Frequently Asked Questions — TMI Studios

TMI Studios is a South African digital playground combining browser games, viral tools, and trending youth culture news. Below are clear answers to common questions about the platform.

What is TMI Studios?

TMI Studios is an African-built digital platform featuring instant browser games, chaos quizzes, experimental viral tools, and trending South African youth culture news — all accessible without downloads.

Is TMI Studios a news website?

TMI Studios includes a dedicated news section covering South African youth culture, entertainment, lifestyle, viral moments, and emerging technology. It is not limited to tech-only news.

Do I need to download anything?

No. All games, tools, and experiences on TMI Studios run directly in your browser on both mobile and desktop.

What kind of games are in the Arcade?

The Arcade features fast casual browser games, party-style quizzes, and chaotic social experiences built for quick play and sharing.

What is the Lab section?

The Lab hosts experimental tools like meme generators, quote creators, fortune cookies, and other viral web experiments designed for creativity.

Is TMI Studios free?

Yes. TMI Studios is free to explore and use. Some future features may include premium or sponsored experiences.

Who is TMI Studios for?

TMI Studios is built for African youth, creators, gamers, and anyone who enjoys internet culture, interactive content, and viral experiences.
